Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed bananas (3 bananas)
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar.
  3. Add the eggs, vanilla, and bananas. Mix together.
  4. Add dry ingredients and mix.
  5. Mix in the pecans.
  6. Bake in two greased loaf pans for about 40 minutes, until skewer comes out clean.
