Ingredients

  • Chickpeas

  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • Olive oil
  • 1/3 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tbs butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbs honey

  • Salad

  • Mixed greens lettuce
  • Halved cherry tomatoes
  • Avocado chunks
  • Diced red onion
  • Blue cheese
  • Spicy ranch dressing

Directions

  1. Combine chickpeas and olive oil on baking pan; roast at 450°F for 20 minutes.
  2. In a small saucepan over low heat, add butter, hot sauce, honey, and salt and pepper.
  3. Toss chickpeas and sauce together; there will be sauce left over.
  4. Assemble the salad.
