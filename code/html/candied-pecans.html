Ingredients

  • 1 lb pecan halves
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tbs water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp cinamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 250°F
  2. Whisk egg white and watter for about 20 seconds, until foamy.
  3. In a large bowl, mix pecans with egg white mixture.
  4. Add sugar and cinnamon. Mix until everything is evenly distributed.
  5. Pour pecans onto parchment lined baking sheet and arrange into a relatively flat layer.
  6. Bake for 1 hour, tossing every 15 minutes.
