Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu, drained and pressed, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tbs corn starch
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Toss all ingredients in a large bowl, then transfer to baking pan.
  3. Bake for 25-30 minutes, tossing tofu halfway through, until tofu is very dark golden brown and crispy.
