Ingredients

  • 2 lbs good quality tomatoes
  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • 1 cucumber, peeled
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbs red wine vinegar
  • 1/3 cup olive oil, plus more for croutons
  • 1/2 loaf crusty French bread, cubed
  • 6 or so basil leaves, ribboned
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cut all vegetables roughly so that they will ffit into a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add red wine vinegar, olive oil, salt and pepper.
  3. Blend again, taste, and adjust seasoning.
  4. Refridgerate until chilled.
  5. Meanwhile, put cubed bread into large skilllet and generously coat with olive oil.
  6. Toast over medium heat until most sides are some shade of golden brown (dark or light).
  7. Season with salt and pepper and let cool.
  8. To serve, ladle into bowls and top with heap of croutons and basil ribbons.
