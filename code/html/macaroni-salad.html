Ingredients

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 4 stalks celery, sliced thin
  • 2 small yellow onions, chopped
  • 12 petite sweet gherkins
  • 4 hard boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cup mayo
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/8 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1-2 grated carrots

Directions

  1. Cook macaroni to al dente.
  2. Once macaroni is cooked, add all ingredients to a large bowl and mix thoroughly.
  3. Season to taste and refridgerate.

Notes

  • Add olive oil to water before adding macaroni to prevent sticking
