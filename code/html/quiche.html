Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust
  • 4-5 eggs
  • 1 cup half and half, or 1/2 cup each of whole milk and heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 2 cups add-ins

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Blind bake chilled pie crust for 15-20 minutes. Use a foil ring or pie curst shield to prevent burning, and baking weights.
  3. Reduce oven temperature to 350°F.
  4. Whisk eggs and milk together, then add salt, pepper, and any herbs.
  5. Add egg mix to crust, then scatter cheese and add-ins evenly.
  6. Bake for 1 hour.

Notes

  • Dry beans can be used as a substitute for baking weights.
  • Add-ins that are low in moisture tend to work best, as a high moisture content can cause the bottom of the crust to go soggy.
  • Some ideas for add-ins: mushroom and onion with cheddar/goat cheese, Greek (spinach, sauteed kalamata olive halves, feta cheese)
