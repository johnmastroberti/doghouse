Buttermilk Drop Biscuits

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Yield: 8-10 biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsps baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk, very cold
  • 1 stick butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Melt the buttter and allow to cool slightly while other ingredients are prepped.
  3. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  4. In a large bowl, mix together dry ingredients.
  5. Place cold buttermilk in a small bowl.
  6. While stirring, pour butter in a slow stream into buttermilk. Once fully mixed, mixture will look curdled.
  7. Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients; do not overmix!
  8. Drop 8-10 mounds of dough onto baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 13-15 minutes, until tops are speckled with dark golden brown spots.

Notes

  • If serving plain, brush with butter and serve warm.
