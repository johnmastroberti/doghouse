Ingredients

  • Filling

  • 2 1/4 lbs dark cherries, pitted (whole)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup corn starch
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/8 cup orange juice

  • Crumb Topping

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp cinamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 stick softened butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Pit cherries by removing stem and piercing where stem was to push pit out the bottom using a metal or hard plastic straw.
  3. Combine all filling ingredients in large bowl, then pour into 2 1/2 quart casserole dish.
  4. Combine all topping ingredients in medium bowl. Break topping into small pieces and cover filling.
  5. Bake for ?? minutes, until you can see filling bubbling.
