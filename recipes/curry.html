Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 large/2 small yellow onions, diced
  • 1 small head cauliflower, in florets
  • 14 oz can coconut milk
  • 14 oz can tomatoes (sauce or crushed)
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cashews, roasted
  • big handful of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup shredded unsweetened coconut

Directions

  1. Toss cauliflower with enough olive oil, salt, and pepper to lightly coat. Roast at 425°F.
  2. Coat a large sautee pan with olive oil over medium high heat.
  3. Add the seeds and cook for one minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Add the onion and cook until translucent, then add the garlic, cooking another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the coconut milk, tomato sauce, spices, shredded coconut, and cauliflower.
  6. Reduce heat to low, let simmer down about 20 minutes, or until desired thickness is reached.
  7. Once done, stir in tomatoes, lime, cashews, and cilantro.
  8. Season and serve over rice with crispy baked tofu.
