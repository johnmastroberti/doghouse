Ingredients

  • 1 small/medium eggplant, peeled and cubed into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 zuchinni, cut into 1/2 moons
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 lb penne or other shape
  • Olive oil
  • 1 tbs finely chopped fresh oregano
  • 2 tbs finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 basil leaves, cut into ribbons
  • 1 ball mozzarella diced into 1 cm cubes
  • 1/2 cup parmesan, plus more for serving
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Place vegetables on pans. Toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast tomatoes and zuchinni on one pan for 10 minutes, tossing halfway.
  4. Roast eggplant on another pan for 15 minutes, tossing halfway.
  5. Meanwhile, cook penne to al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  6. Drain penne and return to pot. Add parmesan and half of pasta water. Stir to combine.
  7. Add vegetables, herbs, mozarella, salt and pepper. Stir again, adding more pasta water if more "sauciness" is desired.
  8. Serve in bowls topped with extra parmesan.
