Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • Roasted tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella (fresh or shredded)
  • Cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • Red onion, sliced
  • Fresh basil ribbons
  • Salt and pepper
  • Dried oregano
  • Fresh garlic, sliced thin
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Olive oil
  • Reduced balsamic vinegar (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Slice the baguette into thirds, then halve longways to make six equal pieces.
  3. Drizzle baguette slices with olive oil, then toast until beginning to turn golden around the edges, about 5 minutes.
  4. Top with sauce, mozarella, tomatoes, red onion slices, and garlic.
  5. Bake for 7 minutes.
  6. If desired, serve with balsamic vinegar drizzled over the top.

Notes

  • If you're using fresh mozarella, use less than you think you need.
